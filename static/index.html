About:

Welcome to Turtles VS Dragons! This game is a side scrolling version of Space Invaders! It's up to you to stop those evil dragons from destroying the entire swamp on the turtles. Use your magic bullets to destroy as many of them as you can!

Instructions:

Above the game window you will see different buttons and game information. The dropdown menu will let you select what kind of background you would like to see when shooting the dragons. Nothing special happens besides just changing how things look. You can also choose to open up the instructions again if you forgot how to do something.

For this game, you will start with 20 lives. Every time you kill a certain amount of dragons your life counter will increase. However, this will only increase up to a certain point.

Your score is also determined by how many dragons you kill. As you kill more dragons, you will eventually hit checkpoints that will increase how many points you will earn. However, you this will only increase up to a certain point.

In addition to see the amount of points and lives you have, you will also be able to see how many dragons you have killed. If you play enough you will be able to tell just how many dragons you need to kill before you hit each checkpoint. However, as you gain bonuses for the amount of dragons you kill, they dragons speed and the frequency they spawn at will also increase.

Controls:

The controls are pretty simple for this game. W is up, S is down, A is left, and D is right. You can only go a certain distance to your left, but as you move around don't be surpised if something unexpected happens. Oh and before I forget, just hit the spacebar if you want to shoot the dragons. Good Luck!
